CAPABILITIES
KARUR HUB

  • High-Tensile Tubular Steel Fabrication Precision cold-formed tubular steel frames cut, bent, and welded to tolerance. Every frame passes load-cycle and weld-integrity inspection before assembly release.
  • High-Density PUF Ergonomic Mapping PUF densities from 30D to 45D, mapped to passenger anthropometric data for each model tier. Foam hardness and rebound are tested per production batch.
  • In-House Upholstery Unit Custom colourways, OEM branding panels, embossed logos, and fleet-uniform finishes. UV-stabilised, flame-retardant certified fabrics tested to 200,000 rub cycles.
  • Recline Mechanism Assembly Smooth multi-stage mechanical recline systems assembled and cycle-tested in-house. Fleet-grade for maximum uptime and minimal maintenance intervention.
Lean Operations Framework
Zero-Queue Assembly
Single-piece flow across all production cells. No batch buildup. Every unit traceable from raw frame to finished seat.
Stress-Test Protocol
Rigid structural integrity testing on every frame lot. Load-cycle simulation before production clearance.
Prescriptive Modelling
Seat geometry derived from mathematical optimisation models, not estimates. Ergonomic mapping updated per OEM requirement.
